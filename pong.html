  The games I made have the only purpose of showing me how the canvas works. And it's really kind of fun thing to play with :D .

  This is the second canvas game made by me. It is my version of the classic game Pong. I made the game only for one player but you have an extra - two options : To play with the mouse , or with the arrow keys. So give it a try. Find out what suits you best - the mouse or the keys :)).

  So the rules are very simple: You play with the blue pad. The goal is not letting the red ball hit the wall behind your pad. The player starts with 5 lifes. Each time the ball hits the wall behind your pad , you lose one life. Each time you achieve to reflect the red ball of your pad , one point is added to your score. The limitations of the ball are the borders of the canvas. Hope you like it. Good luck and have fun :) !

The game will start few seconds after you click the "Start Game !" button ... ;)
