  The games I made have the only purpose of showing me how the canvas works. And it's really kind of fun thing to play with :D .

  The first canvas game made by me is Space Cubes. Yeah I know , it doesn't look like space game , it's more like "space" game , but games are made for developing the imagination and the creativity of the player. My game really needs a little bit more of you imagination so go on , give it a try :)).

  So the rules are simple: You play with the white cube / I know it's a square but the game is 2D , so like I said - use your imagination :D / the enemies are the red cubes and the bottom of the canvas the circles are only for your distraction. Keep away your cube from them and you will receive a point at every time the red cubes misses your cube and leave the borders of the canvas. Good luck and have fun :) !

The game will start few seconds after you click the "Start Game !" button ... ;)
